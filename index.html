How It's Done

5-Step
Process.

From raw aluminium to packaging-ready finished product. Every step inspected.

01

RAW MATERIAL

Aluminium / metal parts received, sorted, weighed and tagged.

02

PRE-TREATMENT

Chemical cleaning to remove oil, grease, rust contamination from the surface.

03

POWDER COATING

Electrostatic spray application in our Back-2-Back operator booth with double cyclone.

04

OVEN CURING

Indirect heating with natural air flow. 20-ft aluminium section capacity.

05

PACKAGING

Quality-checked, packed and ready for collection or dispatch.
